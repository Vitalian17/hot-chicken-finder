While we don’t know all of the details that led up to that faithful night, we do know that after a night of partying, a man’s jaded lover wanted revenge!

Instead of a lecture the next morning, the man awoke to the sizzlin’ smell of fried chicken. The scorned woman created the perfect recipe in an effort to get her revenge.

With the trap set, the man’s jilted lover served up a plate of homemade fried chicken. Without noticing the devilish amount of peppers and spices she had sprinkled on the chicken, he dug in.

Much to her dismay, the man didn’t fall over weeping in pain. Nope, he asked for seconds, and, at that moment, the legend was born.
